CareNav Health

Next.js · TypeScript · GCP · scikit-learn · OpenAI · OCR

End-to-end healthcare navigation platform connecting four integrated ML pipelines: an OCR extraction layer (Mindee) that parses insurance cards and fuzzy-matches against 20,354 CMS plan records in BigQuery; a GPT-4 symptom triage engine returning urgency, CPT codes, and ICD-10 matches via cosine similarity over 2,250 embeddings; a GradientBoostingRegressor cost model (R²=0.92, MAE=$17) trained on the CMS 2024 Physician Fee Schedule with facility-type and plan-type multipliers; and a RAG benefits advisor grounding GPT-4 responses strictly on matched plan JSON. Provider results are ranked by a composite cost-efficiency, rating, and distance score with z-score outlier detection.

Machine Learning & Data Science

Psychedelic Neural Fingerprinting

Neuroimaging · fMRI · SVM · UMAP · Python

Built a complete neuroimaging ML pipeline across two public fMRI datasets (N=22). Processed raw NIfTI and CIFTI files through Schaefer 100-ROI parcellation, computed functional connectivity matrices, and trained an SVM classifier achieving AUC=0.967 under strict leave-one-subject-out cross-validation (permutation p=0.001). Key result: classifier trained on LSD vs. placebo generalized to psilocybin vs. methylphenidate across datasets (AUC=0.918), confirming a pharmacologically-specific connectivity signature. Longitudinal analysis revealed sustained post-psilocybin brain reorganization not observed after stimulant controls.

Cognitive Fatigue Analyzer

Behavioral DS · Streamlit · Random Forest · Plotly

Designed and deployed an interactive ML app modeling cognitive decision fatigue from physiological and behavioral signals. Engineered 4 derived features grounded in cognitive load research — sleep deficit, decision density, cognitive pressure, and fatigue risk index — then trained a Random Forest classifier (96.7% accuracy, 5-fold CV) and regressor (R²=0.998, RMSE=1.68) over 25,000 simulated decision states. The Streamlit app delivers real-time fatigue scoring, population percentile comparison, 3 recovery scenario projections, and a 5-hour forward trajectory model.

Publications

Effects of Simulated Hypo-Gravity on Lower Limb Kinematics & EMG

Frontiers in Physiology · June 2023

Investigated how gravitational unloading history shapes lower-limb kinematics and EMG during anti-gravity treadmill walking across loads from 100% to 20% body weight. Found that prior loading level significantly alters neuromuscular responses at the same current load — particularly in the medial gastrocnemius and rectus femoris — with implications for rehabilitation protocols and space physiology. Data collected at the Center for Neuromotor and Biomechanics Research, University of Houston.
